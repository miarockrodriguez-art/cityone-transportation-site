A Company Built on Trust

Cityone Transportation was founded in Orlando, Florida in 1999 with a singular purpose: to provide Central Florida's visitors and residents with ground transportation that is professional, reliable, and genuinely attentive to every passenger.

What began as a focused operation serving Orlando International Airport has grown into one of the region's most respected transportation companies — serving every major hotel corridor, cruise terminal, theme park, attraction, and airport in Central Florida.

Our fleet of SUVs, vans, and motor coaches is maintained to the highest standards. But our greatest asset has always been our people — drivers and staff who treat every client with the dignity and professionalism that has built our reputation over 25 years.

Cityone Transportation is family-owned and operated. That is not simply a description — it is a reflection of how we approach every interaction, every reservation, and every ride.
